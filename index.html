projects()

GUESS THE QUOTE

I made a web app using SvelteKit and firebase. It is a game where you have to guess the author of a quote. It has user authentication, a browse page where you can search for publich stacks of quotes and a home page where you can create and play your own stacks of quotes.

NUMBERTYPE

This is my final project for CS50x. It is a website with which a user can memorize their contacts' phone numbers and practice typing numbers on their keyboard. I made it using SvelteKit, Auth.js and Prisma.

UNIT CONVERTER

My first project, arguably my least impressive, was a basic unit converter created using Python and Customtkinter. It facilitates conversion among different units of length, weight, temperature, volume, and pressure. Additionally, it features a dark mode.

STATIFY

Statify is a website where users can view their spotify statistics. It is made using SvelteKit and the spotify API. Users can login with their spotify account and view their top artists, tracks and genres. It uses Tailwind CSS for styling and utilizes Chart.js to present the genres data in a visually appealing manner.

thoughts()

Will AI replace programmers?

No, I don't think so, but I also believe that AI will become a mandatory tool, enabling programmers to concentrate on the interesting and complex aspects of their job.

A Programmer can't do everything

I feel like many people who don't program have this impression that every programmer can hack into whatever they want, create a complex multiplayer game by themselves in a week, or develop a Twitter replacement in a day. It's important to keep in mind that there's a reason why there are thousands of people working at these big tech companies. A programmer isn't a 100-person army.

Why you should give Neovim/Vim a try.

Before Neovim, I, like many, heavily relied on VSCode shortcuts for efficiency. However, finding VSCode limiting, I tried a Vim extension, and the speed boost was impressive. During a slow laptop vacation, I switched to Neovim and never looked back. Returning home, I made Neovim my default for its superior experience and increased productivity. I believe everyone should give Neovim or Vim a try; it can genuinely enhance productivity and is a fun tool to use as well.
